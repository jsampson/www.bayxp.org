Rod McChesney has been developing software professionally since 1982. He’s worked in a variety of areas for companies including Tibco, Sun Microsystems (including SunSoft and JavaSoft), NOCPulse, and Openwave. He’s currently at at Postini (soon to be a Google subsidiary) working as the technical lead for the web applications group.

At NOCpulse he worked on a SaaS system monitoring product that was developed with a home-rolled agile approach, delivering updates every three to four weeks. Having seen how well this can work in practice, he’s tried to bring the ideas to teams at other companies, with varying success. The Postini web applications team has been using its own agile approach for about a year, and using XPlanner for about the last six months.
